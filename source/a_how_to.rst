How to
======

The 'how to' sections introduce you to specific 3Di capabilities and explain how you can get the most out of them. Rather than specific step-by-step instructions, the 'how to' sections are guidelines for making the right choices in your specific use case.

If want to become familiar with the basics of working with 3Di and its common workflows, please check out the :ref:`e_tutorials` section.
